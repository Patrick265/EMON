Use of the application

In Insight knowledge you can see the data from multiple smartsensor of students from Avans Breda.
In the list/mainpage all sensors are available. When clicked an insights page with usefull information will load with all the records from the smartsensor.
On the overview page all the energy stats, which are manditory for every smartmeter, are compared into one chart.
